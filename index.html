Who are we?

We are a student tutoring service that offers a modern holstic model of tutoring at competative prices. We sought to integrate modern technologies to supplement traditional in person tutoring, connecting the student-mentor dialogue anywhere and anytime.

Our tutors are qualified college students or graduates. Join our community on Slack to ask questions and learn more!

Here's our core tools

    • Zoom, for screensharing and video calls to supplement learning online and on mobile.

    • Google Drive, for file sharing and uploading textbooks and SAT practice tests for online access.

    • Slack, for instant text communication that supports code sharing, asking questions to other students and tutors, and file sharing.

    • Calendy, for flexible scheduling and rescheduling, to see availability times for available tutors, so you don't forget.

    • Toggl, for accurately tracking studying time and keeping pace with study goals.

    • Data analysis, to build a customized curiculum that targets weaknesses while developing fundementals.
